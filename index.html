| Tokenomics

$CatNap has a total supply of 1,000,000,000,000,000 $CATNAP tokens, crafted with precise tokenomics designed to incentivize holders and foster a healthy coin environment:

Initial Liquidity: 40%

Community Airdrop: 30%

Future Development and Partnerships: 10%

Team Allocation: 10%

Reserve: 10%

Furthermore, each transaction is taxed 5%, divided as follows:

Burned: 1%

Distributed to holders: 2%

Added back to the liquidity pool: 2%

With no presale and an anti-whale mechanism, we ensure a fair and resilient coin.
